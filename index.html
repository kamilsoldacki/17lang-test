How to run a session

  1. Pick your native language — evaluate only languages you speak natively or near-natively.
  2. Start the call and just talk, like on a casual phone call — mix Telekom topics (billing, roaming, plans) with smalltalk (weather, travel, your day). Try to get the assistant to say prices, dates and app names — that's where accents slip first.
  3. Aim for 5+ minutes, then end the call and run one more session — the voice varies naturally between generations, so never judge from a single call.
  4. You are rating how the voice sounds, not what it says — the assistant invents prices and details on purpose. Ignore factual content entirely.
  5. After your sessions, fill in the rating form: [link to rating form].
